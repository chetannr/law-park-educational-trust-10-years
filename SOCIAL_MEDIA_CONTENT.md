# Social Media Content - Bharat Shiksha Ratan Award

## 🏆 Award Information
- **Award:** Bharat Shiksha Ratan Award
- **Recipient:** Charulatha M. R. (Founder, Law Park Educational Trust)
- **Organization:** Economic and Social Development Foundation (E.S.D.F)
- **Date:** 19th December 2025
- **Location:** New Delhi
- **Presented By:** Union Ministers - Mr. Kamal Singh Negi (Cabinet Minister) & Mr. Dinesh Upadhyay (Ayush Minister)
- **Total Awardees:** 25 (Law Park Educational Trust was one of the honored recipients)
